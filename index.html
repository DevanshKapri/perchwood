The Jungle Café
at Perchwood Khatima

#1 Family Restaurant in Khatima

The Jungle Café is more than just a dining experience—it's a journey into the heart of the forest. Our menu features a delightful mix of local and international cuisine, prepared with the freshest ingredients. Enjoy your meal in a serene environment, surrounded by the sights and sounds of the forest. Whether you’re here for a hearty breakfast, a leisurely lunch, or an intimate dinner, The Jungle Café promises an unforgettable dining experience.

The Gliding Rest Café

The Gliding Rest Café & Restaurant is a celebration of the rich culinary traditions of Uttarakhand, blended with global flavors. Our menu is crafted from locally sourced ingredients, ensuring every dish is fresh and flavorful. Experience the real Uttarakhandi culture the way it’s supposed to be, whether you’re enjoying a meal on our scenic terrace or in the cozy, beautifully designed dining area. At The Gliding Rest, every meal is a journey through the tastes and traditions of the hills.
