Hotel Perchwood

Luxury Hotel in Khatima & Bhimtal

Experience the warmth and comfort of home at Perchwood Hotel, offering the best homestay in Bhimtal. Our homestay accommodations provide a cozy and welcoming environment, perfect for families and travelers looking for a homely atmosphere. Located in the serene hills of Bhimtal, our homestay offers a unique blend of traditional hospitality and modern amenities, ensuring a memorable and comfortable stay.

At Perchwood Hotel, we understand the importance of creating a home-away-from-home experience for our guests. Our homestay rooms are designed to offer maximum comfort and convenience, with spacious interiors, comfortable bedding, and all the necessary amenities. Whether you're traveling with family, friends, or as a couple, our homestay accommodations provide the perfect setting for a relaxing and enjoyable stay.

Bhimtal is a beautiful and tranquil destination, known for its picturesque lake and lush green surroundings. Staying at our homestay allows you to fully immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the area while enjoying the comforts of home. From leisurely walks around Bhimtal Lake to exploring the nearby attractions, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Our homestay's prime location ensures that you can easily access all the activities and sights that Bhimtal has to offer.

Our on-site dining options offer a variety of delicious home-cooked meals, prepared with fresh local ingredients. Whether you're in the mood for traditional Indian dishes or international cuisine, our talented chefs create meals that satisfy the palate and nourish the soul. Enjoy your meal in our cozy dining area or take advantage of our room service for a more private dining experience. Our homely food is sure to make you feel right at home.

In addition to comfortable accommodations and delicious food, we pride ourselves on offering exceptional service. Our friendly and attentive staff are always on hand to assist with any needs or special requests. Whether you need help planning your activities or recommendations for the best places to visit in Bhimtal, we are here to help. Our goal is to ensure that your stay at Perchwood Hotel is as enjoyable and memorable as possible.

Discover why Perchwood Hotel is considered the best homestay in Bhimtal. Book your stay with us today and experience the perfect combination of homely comfort, natural beauty, and outstanding hospitality. We look forward to welcoming you and making your visit truly special.
