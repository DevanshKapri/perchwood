About Us

Immerse Yourself In A Restful Haven

Hotel Perchwood was born from the desire to redesign the notion of hospitality experiences in exceptional destinations. Welcome to the finest hospitality service provider in the middle of serene and tranquil beauty of Khatima. Ideally located between flaura and fauna and within reach of local market, Hotel Perchwood gives beautiful glimpses of heavenly nature. The hotel offers exotic mouth watering cuisines with culinary prowess for its guests.

Come, indulge in nature and let fresh crispy tranquil and serene air nurture you to the core.
